Get in touch

By phone

In an on-campus emergency, call Campus Safety at (484) 664-3110 or dial 3110 from a campus phone. The non-emergency line for Campus Safety is (484) 664-3112, or 3112 from campus phones.

Online

You can reach us by email at bergems@muhlenberg.edu, and find us on Instagram as Berg.EMS.

For inquiries regarding CPR classes, please email bergems@muhlenberg.edu.

By mail

Our mailing address is:

Muhlenberg College EMS
Department of Campus Safety
Muhlenberg College
2400 West Chew Street
Allentown, PA 18104

In person

If you need to drop off paperwork, bring it to Campus Safety in the basement of Prosser Hall and ask for it to be placed in our mailbox.

Medical Records

To request your medical records, please contact the Muhlenberg College Health Center.
